Servant Leadership in Practice

Introduction

Effective leadership requires a combination of vision, empathy, and decisive action. Leaders who demonstrate vulnerability often build stronger teams than those who project invincibility. According to Goleman (1995), emotional intelligence accounts for nearly 90 percent of what distinguishes star performers from average ones. The transformational leadership model emphasizes inspiring followers through a shared vision rather than relying on transactional reward systems.
